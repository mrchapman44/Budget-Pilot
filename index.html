Why do I see a "Google hasn't verified this app" warning?

Don't worry, Pilot. This is a standard Google safety check for any spreadsheet that uses custom automation scripts. Because you are the owner of the sheet, you are simply giving your own copy permission to run the "Auto-Pilot" code. We provide a step-by-step guide in the Flight Manual to bypass this in 3 clicks.

Does this work in Microsoft Excel?

Negative. Budget Pilot is engineered specifically for Google Sheets to take advantage of Google Apps Script for the bank import automation. It will not function in Excel.

Can you see my financial data?

Never. Your data stays on your Google Drive. We have no access to your sheet, your bank CSVs, or your spending habits. You are the sole controller of your data.
